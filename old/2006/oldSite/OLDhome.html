The Artemis Project is a five week summer day camp for rising 9th grade girls in the Providence area. It is run by four Brown undergraduate women, in connection with Brown University Computer Science.

Artemis is designed to encourage young women in science and technology. The students will learn both concrete computer skills and abstract computer science concepts through a variety of projects and activities, in a positive and encouraging environment. Click here for more information.
