The Artemis 2011 Curriculum!

Week 1: June 27-June 31

Computer-based:

  • Microsoft Excel: Grade Calculator
  • Microsoft PowerPoint: About Me PowerPoint
  • Adobe Photoshop: Name Tag, Face in Hole, Movie Poster
Non computer-based:
  • Graph Theory (Part 1): Intro to Graphs, Euler Circuit & Path
  • Graph Theory (Part 2): Prim's & Kruskal's MST Algorithms
  • Graph Theory (Part 3): Graph Coloring
  • Graph Theory (Part 4): Minimum Dominating Sets

Week 2: July 5-July 8

Computer-based:

  • Web Design: HTML & CSS
Non computer-based:
  • Binary, NIM strategy
  • Logic Gates

Week 3: July 11-July 15

Computer-based:

  • Scratch: Intro to Programming, Etch-a-Sketch, Final Projects
Non computer-based:
  • Sorting and Binary Search
  • Search Algorithms: Breadth-First Search, Depth-First Search
  • Recursion: Towers of Hanoi

Week 4: July 18-July 22

Computer-based:

  • Python: Syntax, Madlibs, Encoder/Decoder, Graphics
Non computer-based:
  • Cryptography
  • Phylogenetics

Week 5: July 25-July 29

Computer-based:

  • Robotics: Programming Robots in Scratch
Non computer-based:
  • Preparation for Final Presentations
