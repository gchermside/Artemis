Whether you are overweight, underweight, or simply care about your health, "Fresh and Fit" is the perfect app for you.

The Hard Truth

  • Today two-thirds of adults and nearly one-third of children struggle with overweight and obesity
  • Obesity is linked to more than 60 chronic diseases
  • 72% of older men and 67% of older women are now overweight or obese
  • Being underweight can lead to delayed growth and development
  • Those who are not nourishing themsleves enough have been shown to lack energy, have depression, heart irregularities, and anemia.
  • Bottom line: your health is nothing to be played with.
