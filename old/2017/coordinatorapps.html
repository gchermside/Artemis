Want to be an Artemis Coordinator?

Apply Here

Applicants must be Brown University undergraduates who identify as female and have studied some Computer Science. Students currently in their first semester of an intro sequence are qualified and encouraged to apply! This is a competitively-paid position that has a spring semester component.

Artemis coordinators are selected for a variety of strengths and skills, including independence, ability to both contribute ideas and to build off the ideas of others, teaching experience, organizational skills, and creativity. Artemis coordinators are responsible for both administrative and academic aspects of a university summer program. They recruit and evaluate applicants, reserve classroom space, arrange lunches, and organize field trips and speaker events. They also develop, write, and teach five weeks' worth of curriculum. It is particularly important that applicants be able to carry out job responsibilities without supervision. Applicants should be self-motivated and capable of figuring out what needs to be done, then doing it.
