Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Inspired by the hit-show Shark Tank, this summer's computer science curriculum will feature a strong emphasis on women in business. The girls will be split into teams, and throughout the summer they will be working to complete three major tasks: Creating an Idea, Coding the Backbone, and Drawing up a Business Plan. The Artemis Coordinators and TAs will work to assist them through each of these stages, but in the end, it's up to the girls to decide how they will pitch their idea at the end of the summer.

We spend part of each day in a computer lab learning computer literacy, programming, and business concepts through the use of programs like Microsoft Excel, Adobe Photoshop, AppInventor, Python, and HTML/CSS. We also spend part of each day in a classroom where students will be introduced to the more theoretical aspects of computer science. During this portion of the day, we cover topics such as Cryptography and Artificial Intelligence through games and other hands-on activities.
