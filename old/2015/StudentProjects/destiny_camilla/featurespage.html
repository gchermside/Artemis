CheckBox is the interactive planner app that offers even more features than your paper planner. At the moment, these include a list to write all your tasks in, and a timer that will help you get those tasks done. The creators of CheckBox plan to include many more features in future updates, such as a calendar where you can insert important dates and an account feature, so you can access the assignments you've completed and the ones you have yet to do from any device. Also, they would like to implement features specifically for students, so you can study in more dynamic ways. These include a calculator, a dictionary and thesaurus, flash cards, and a periodic table. We hope you are enjoying the app as it is, but it will get even better from here!
