CheckBox was created in the summer of 2015 by two rising high school freshmen, Camilla Ledezma and Destiny Manston. They were both attending a computer science program at Brown University called The Artemis Project at the time. This camp helped them develop the skills and knowledge they needed to code an app (and make this website for it). Once they found out that they would be working together, it didn't take long for them to come up with the idea for CheckBox. After all, they were procrastinators themselves and they had always wanted something like CheckBox to help them get things done. Thankfully, with help from the camp coordinators, Camilla and Destiny were able to turn their idea into an actual app in just a few weeks. Since then, they have continued to add new features to CheckBox in hopes that the users will enjoy it even more and be able to manage their time in a more efficient way.
