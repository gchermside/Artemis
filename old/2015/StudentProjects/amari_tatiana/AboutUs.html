Pure Allure is a beauty app, that helps you bulid confidence. As two teenage girls Amari Newby and Tatiana Delgado thought it would be a fun and helpful experience to create an app on this topic. This app was inspired by many other beauty apps such as 'The Glam App', 'Spruce', and 'Glamsquad'. We aspired to make this app to help build confidence in others and to make finding and buying products easier.
