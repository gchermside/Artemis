  • 'Make sure you hydrate the skin and put on a primer.'- Jessie Powers
  • Always use a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • 'When you wear a red lip it is important that it stays exactly where you put it. Using a long wearing lip pencil to create the perfect shape and a lip color are key to having a perfect red lip.'-Kristofer Buckle
  • Remember, clean your makeup brushes to fight against potential bacteria and breakouts! At least once a week, swirl brushes around in a mixture of warm water and mild soap, then lay flat to dry.
  • Winter can be especially harsh on your hair leaving it extra dry. Eugene Davis, celebrity hair stylist suggests using an avocado oil hair mask.
  • During the summer, wear lip balm or gloss with SPF.
  • If you are not a fan of liquid liner, get the look of liquid eye liner by using a gel instead.
  • To minimize pores, use a pore-minimizing facial wash followed by a toner. Make sure the toner is alchohol-free so it does not dry out your skin.
  • To contour cheeks, use a matte powder (a few shades darker than your skin tone) with an angled brush. Blend on temples and under cheek bone.
  • To help you find the right shade of foundation, choose the product you think is closest to your color and try it on your jawline in natural light. You will be able to easily compare it to the color on your neck to see if you need to go for a different undertone, lighter or darker.
  • To reduce unwanted shine, use a moisturizer that mattifies your skin. That way you get the moisture your skin needs without looking greasy.
  • 'After using a a hair conditioning mask, follow with a cold water rinse to close the hair cuticles'- Ursala Stephensen
  • Hydrate your under-eye area with an eye cream to prevent puffiness and bags.
  • Mascara and eyeliner products need to be replaced every 3 to 4 months to prevent bacteria bulid up.
  • After you paint your nails put them in a bowl of ice-cold water to help them dry faster.
  • Workout and eat healthy on a regular basis,remember healthy habits equals beautiful you.
