In order to eliminate inappropriate language from various websites, WordBlot checks the text in a website, and recreates a clean version. The clean version will not include any violent, offensive or crude words and/or phrases.

Original post WordBlot Version
"I *swear* love Netflix!" "I love Netflix!"
"You suck." "You."
"I'm going to kill you." "Im going to you."

The WordBlot version is safer for kids to read than the original!
