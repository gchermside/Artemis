What is it?

Today, everyone surfs the internet and can post just about anything, making many sites inappropriate for young children. You wouldn't want your child to see that material without your permission, would you? WordBlot is designed to act as a childlock for when you aren't around to surf the web with your children. The program filters through websites and removes innappropriate language, making the internet a safer place for kids!

Settings

WordBlot also has parental security, so that children cannot change the settings. The program asks for a password so that only you can access it. Another feature lets you customize which phrases and words you want to remove from various websites.

A Little More Info

WordBlot was created as a final product for the Artemis Project, a five week program at Brown University that empowers young women to learn about computer science. Click on "About Us" to learn more about the cofounders.
