The MeHarmony app is simple and easy to use. All you have to do is input whatever note in which you want to find a harmony for and then your output will be every note that harmonizes with your note. They come out in a list so that you have the option to choose which one best matches the mood of your song, or which one you like the best.

My goals are for this app to be a good tool for people who love to write music and also a good learning tool for anyone who wants to learn how to harmonize. Also, I would love for people to be so impressed with the app that they pass it on to someone else so that more people can learn and use it for whatever they might need it for.
