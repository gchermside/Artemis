  • About Me

Imagine you are a songwriter. You have a great idea for a tune. You have your melody in mind, but you feel as though the dimension of your melody is flat. What are you missing? A harmony, but finding harmonies is definitely not your strong suit. This is where the MeHarmony comes in to play. I created an app for helping singer/songwriters harmonize their tunes without the hassle of having to spend a lot of their precious time doing it themselves. The MeHarmony app is here to help and equipped with everything you need to find perfect harmony. Who knows? Maybe our harmonizing app will bring some harmony to your life as well as your melody.

My product is made to help with harmonies. It will help anyone of any age who either wants to just play around with a harmony, or is using it for something serious. If you are not someone who can naturally harmonize, our app will give you suggestions on which note will work with whichever note you input into the app
