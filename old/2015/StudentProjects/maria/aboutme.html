My name is Maria Angelone. I love to write music, but I hated having to take the time to come up with a harmony. When I came up with the idea for MeHarmony, I figured it would be useful for people like me. After getting a second opinion, my eyes were opened to a whole new oportunity for the app. It doesn't only need to be used to help people who already knew about harmonies, but also can be used to educate people who didn't know about them and are willing to learn. The design is simple enough that even if you don't know your way around a sheet of music, you can easily access anything you would need to satisfy your musical needs. I hope you enjoy my app!
