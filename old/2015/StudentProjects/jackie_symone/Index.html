BRAIN STORM is an app to help train your brain to be it's best possible self.

Have you ever felt that you are bit slower on thing like recall than you would like to be? Do you ever have trouble remembering things in your day-to-day life that should be easy such as names? Addresses? Events? These problems are more easy to fix than you might think.

This may sound hard and unnecesary, but what if I told you that it could be fun? Would you rather sit Around watching TV, mindlessly eating snacks and channel surfing, or do something that requires just as little effort and can help you in the long run to reach your full intelectual potential.

BRAIN STORM can help you to hone skills such as focus, recall, reflexes, and an ability to multitask in a way that feels like fun games.

BRAIN STORM is also not at all time consuming. You can reach sucess in as little as a few minutes a day.

Watch you be the first one of your friends to remember the name of that great dish at the new resturaunt downtown. Have them watch in awe as you are never late for another event because you forgot until the very last minute. With BRAIN STORM these things are easy to achieve. What do you have to loose?
