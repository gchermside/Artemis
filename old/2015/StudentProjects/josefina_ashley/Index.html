No more wasting food or simply eating junk foods. We'll help you improve your health while we provide you with healthy recipes. Our app would enable users select the ingredients they have at their home. Pictures of common healthy ingrediants would appear on the screen which would allow users to click on the picture of what they wish to use in their recipe. After selecting the user's chosen ingrediants a quick and easy recipe tailored to the user's choices would appear. Our app would enable user's to eat healthy food and use ingediants they might not have known how to use. As a result they would feel great and waste less food!
