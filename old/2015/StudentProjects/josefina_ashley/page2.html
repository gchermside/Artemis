Smoothies are great options for in between meals instead of snacking on those salty,fatty potato chips. They're also a great way to use up fruit,yogurt or milk that you're not sure what to do with. A simple but delicoius smoothie recipe is as follows: 1 cup of ice 1 cup of spinach 1 cup of frozen peach slices(or fresh peaches whatever you prefer) 1 cup of vanilla almond milk 2 teaspoons of honey. This smoothie recipe is just one example of all healthy, quick, and easy recipes CookEasy provides to its users.
