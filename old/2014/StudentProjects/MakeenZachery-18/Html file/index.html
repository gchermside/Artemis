How her career began

Beyonce is a singer, songwriter, and actress who was born and raised in Houston, Texas on September 4th, 1981. As a child she and her sister (Solange Knowles) participated in various singing and dancing competitions. Her career really began in the early 1990s as the lead singer of the girl group Destiny's Child(a group created by her father, Mathew Knowles.) Their first hit was a song called "No, no, no" released in 1996. For another nine years they released differet hits like: "Soldier", "Survivor", and "Bootylicious".
