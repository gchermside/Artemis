How Hedgehogs are Freaking AMAZING

Hedgehogs have adorable little spines, which make them resemble things like pinecones, cacti, hairbrushes, and sea urchins. Plus, they have the ability to curl up into a little spiky ball when they're scared! How cute is that?! Their spikes don't break easily, and can protect them from falls, predators, and small children. Wow, a pet that protects itself from small children? This little cutie does everything!

Also, hedgehogs eat cat food. No special meal here, just feed your cat and that little spiky ball the same thing. They're active in the evening, so they play with you once you come home from your boring job or annoying school. AND YOU CAN CLEAN THEM WITH A TOOTHBRUSH! TOO MUCH CUUUUUUTE!
