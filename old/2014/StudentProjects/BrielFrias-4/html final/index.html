What Frozen really is

Frozen is an animated Disney movie that I love. It is about an ice queen, Elsa, that runs away from her kingdom after everyone finds out that she has ice powers. Then her sister, Anna, and a random stranger,Kristoff, she met at an ice shop set out to find her.Kristoff has a reindeer named, Sven, which they use to find Elsa. Along the way they meet a talking snowman named Olaf who loved summer and wanted to see what the heat really does. But don't worry the kingdom is in good hands, princess Anna left her fiance, Hans in charge.But, with a suprise twist at the end nothing happens the way you expect it too. Will Elsa be rescued, will Olaf find out what summer really is, and will Anna marry Hans find out by watching the #1 family movie, FROZEN.

Why I like Frozen

Personally, I like Frozen because it teaches kids that even though you are a snowman you can still dream about seeing and living in summer.It also teaches kids that family is forever.
