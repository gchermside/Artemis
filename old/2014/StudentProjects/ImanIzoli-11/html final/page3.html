What They're In

'Paul Wesley is an American actor, director, and former model best known for his roles as Stefan Salvatore in The CW television drama The Vampire Diaries and as Aaron Corbett in the ABC Family miniseries Fallen.'

'Ian Joseph Somerhalder is an American actor and model, best known for playing Boone Carlyle in the TV drama Lost and Damon Salvatore in the TV drama The Vampire Diaries.'

'Dylan O'Brien is an American actor, musician, and director. He co-stars as Stiles in the MTV series Teen Wolf. '

'Joseph Morgan is a British actor. He is best known for his role as Niklaus Mikaelson in The CW's The Vampire Diaries and its spin-off The Originals.'

'Claire Holt is an Australian actress, best known ofor her roles as Emma Gilbert on the televison show H20: Just Add Water. Chastity Meyer in Mean Girls 2, and Rebekah Mikaelson on The CW series The Vampire Diaries and its spin-off series The Originals.'

'Daniel Gillies is a Canadian-born New Zealand actor, best known for his role of Elijah Mikaelson on the television series The Vampire Diaries and its spin-off The Originals, as well as his role Dr. Joel Goran on the Canadian series Saving Hope.'

'Phoebe Tonkin is an Australian actor, best known for her roles as Cleo Sertori in H20: Just Add Water, Fiona Maxwell in Tomorrow, When War Began, Faye Chamberlain in The Secret Circle, and Hayley Marshall in The Vampire Diaries and its spin-off series The Originals.'

'Crystal Reed is an American actress. She is best known for her role as Allison Argent in the MTV series Teen Wolf.'

'Tyler Hoechlin is an American actor who got his big break starring as Tom Hanks' son in the film Road To Perdition. For television he is known for playing the role of Martin Brewer on 7th Heaven and the role of Derek Hale on the MTV series Teen Wolf.'

'Daniel Andrew Sharman is a British actor from Hackney, London best known for his role as Ares in the 2011 fantasy film Immortals and as Isaac Lahey in the MTV supernatural drama series Teen Wolf.'
