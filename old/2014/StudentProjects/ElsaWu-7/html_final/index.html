Hetalia

Hetalia('Weak Italy' in English) is an anime about personified nations through world history, starting from World War I and branching onto different points to past and present. The whole theme is comedic and light hearted, but it does have its moments of being able to pull on the heartstrings. The author of the beloved series is Hidekazu Himaruya, and the fandom(AKA fan kingdom) has idolized him sort of like the king of it all.

This page will be about other animes, but the main focus for the first 3 pages will be Hetalia. Each country is fabulous in their own unique way, and in the dubbed version(that can be found on YouTube or on the Funimation website) each country has his/her accent in a stereotypical way.

The basics that I previously forgot to mention:

  • The anime is based off of county stereotypes and basically laughing about it. Do not take anything personally, its comedy.
  • The early animation is, honestly, not the best. But the newest season(Hetalia: The Beautiful World) has the most amazing animation yet.
  • Everyone makes fun of England's eyebrows and his lack of cooking skill. So don't feel bad if you do as well. Its literally an on going gag.
  • EVERYTHING CAN BE CONSIDERED COMEDY, LITERALLY.
  • Yaoi(look it up) shipping is the mosted shipped thing in the fandom.
