How do you know him?

You most likely know Harry as, "That guy in One Direction." In that case, you are correct. However, there is so much more to him than what the media puts out. He is not a, "Ladies' man", or an, "Idiot. He is actually a really lovely person and treats his fans and family with respect. Hopefully this website will reveal the real him.

This is not the official website of Harry Styles by the way, nor is there even an offical website about him. I am in no way affiliated with One Direction.

Basic facts about him!

  • Birthday: February 1st, 1994 (He's 20 years old.)
  • Birthplace: England.

Born to be a star.

Harry always knew he was interested in music. Ever since he was little, he would be singing along to karaoke. For a matter of fact, he played the mouse in one of his school musicals in elementary school. When he was 16, he was the lead singer of a band called "White Eskimo" with his friends in High School. Harry only then realized he wanted to pursue music in his future when he joined this competition called "Battle of the bands" and he and his band won first place. He absolutely loved the feeling of being on the stage. His mother kept on encouraging him to sign up for the X Factor UK, so when sign ups came around, he signed up as a solo contestant...
