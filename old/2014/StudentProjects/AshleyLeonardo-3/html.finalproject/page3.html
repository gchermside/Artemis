Water country

Do you want to have fun and get wet at the same time...? Well if you do go to Water Country. Water Countrty is a really fun ausment park. If you go there yiour going to have a blast going on all the rides and getting splash with water when you don't know.

Water Country is one of the few amusement parks in the US to allow and encourage its guests to bring picnic lunches into the park. A number of large picnic areas are available for free at various points throughout the park so guests can eat without having to leave.Water Country is largest, cleanest, safest, and friendliest water park!

Fun rides at water country.

  • 1.Triple Giant Slides. Triple Giant Slides is a slide were three of your friends can go on the same time.

  • 2.Racing Rapids. This is were you and your friend can get together and race down the slid to see how went down faster and at the same time have fun.

  • 3. Giant Wave Pool. Giant wave pool is were you can go in and just swim and have fun with a group if friends.

Founder of Water Country.

It opened in 1984 and was owned by the Samuels family until they were bought out by Festival Fun Parks in 2000. The park is now owned by Palace Entertainment and has a number of slides and swimming pools that range for all ages. Water Country, touted as being "New England's Largest, Cleanest, Safest, and Friendliest Waterpark", is located in Portsmouth, New Hampshire, United States
