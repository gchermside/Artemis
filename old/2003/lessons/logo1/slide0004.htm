A Little Logo History
What is LOGO?
• A programming language developed at the MIT Artificial Intelligence Lab
• It’s easy to learn, but once you know the basics you can do extremely complicated things • Logo code has been used in telecommunications, multimedia software and robotics
What is the TURTLE?
• Originally, the turtle was a robotic creature that sat on the floor and was directed to move around by a user typing commands into a computer • Today, it is an icon on the Logo screen (in our version of Logo the turtle is represented by a triangle)
