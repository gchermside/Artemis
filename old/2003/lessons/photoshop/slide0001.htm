Tools…Dissected I
Marquee: make rectangular, elliptical, single row, and single column selections.
Move: moves your selections.
Lasso: make freehand and straight-edged selections
Magic Wand: selects similarly colored areas. Clicking on a  particular color in an image causes all the areas of that color to be selected.
Crop: trims images so that you can cut out whichever part of the image you want to use.
Airbrush: paints soft-edged strokes.
Pencil: paints hard-edged strokes, like a regular pencil would.
Paintbrush: paints brush strokes
Eraser: erases parts of an image to a previously saved state.
Background Eraser: erases areas to transparency by dragging
