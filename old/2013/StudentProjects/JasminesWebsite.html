    String heart/shape
    1. First draw your heart or shape on a big peice of paper.
    2. Then put your peice of paper on what you want the string shape on and hammer nails along the line.
      3. >
    3. DO NOT PUSH THE NAILS ALL THE WAY IN!
    4. Tie a string around one of the nails and knot it
    5. Now you can string the string around the nails. There is not a certain way you are supposed to do it! just string them randomly.
    6. Once you like how the string looks, proceed to outline the heart with the rest of the string. This will make thet outline stick out more so it is easy to see the shape.
    7. Congrats! you've made a one of a kind diy project!
