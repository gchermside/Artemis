Artemis Project: Making Web Pages Rules!

Normal text goes into a paragraph that begins with a p tag, and ends with a slash p tag.

A line break is indicated with a BR tag.


A ruler line is indicated with the tag HR.

Blockquotes allows you to offset text, usually for a quote. It uses a BLOCKQUOTE tag at the beginning, and a slash BLOCKQUOTE as an end tag.

There once was a girl name True,
Who ate a whole bottle of glue.
She turned rather green.
And ruptured her spleen.
And she couldn't go to Artemis that day.
