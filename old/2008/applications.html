How to apply

  1. Download a student application: Microsoft Word or PDF
  2. Download a teacher recommendation form: Microsoft Word or PDF
    * You may need to download Adobe Acrobat Reader in order to read the PDF files.
  3. Send the student application and one teacher recommendation to us via snail mail or e-mail by April 30 (postmarked). Mail is preferred. Please mail applications to:

    The Artemis Project 2008
    Computer Science Department
    Brown University
    Box 1910
    Providence, RI 02912-1910

    Our e-mail address is artemis@cs.brown.edu. Please submit e-mail applications as .doc, .txt, or .rtf files.

    If you must fax an application, our fax number is (401) 863-7657 attn Artemis.
