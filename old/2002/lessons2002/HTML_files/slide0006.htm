Spacing
If you really want a new line, you need a LINE BREAK tag:
<br>
HTML automatically puts one space between everything, and the spaces you add are called FORCED SPACES.  Any time you want an extra space:
&nbsp
Both of these instructions get inserted where you want the LINE BREAK or FORCED SPACE, they do NOT have end tags.
HTML is not smart when it comes to spacing.  If you hit ENTER at the end of a line, it doesn’t know what to do, and just keeps right on going.
