Lists 1
There are 3 basic types of lists
1.   An ordered list – use when you want the items in your
                                    list to be numbered.
This:
<OL>
<LI>Eat breakfast
<LI>Eat lunch
<LI>Eat dinner
</OL>
Will look like:
1.Eat breakfast
2.Eat lunch
3.Eat dinner
