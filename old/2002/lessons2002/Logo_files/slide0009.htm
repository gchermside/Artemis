The REPEAT Command
You can get your turtle to do one (or several) things repeatedly, without typing them again and again using the REPEAT command.
Can you think of a way the REPEAT command would be useful for drawing a triangle, square, or pentagon?
This example will move the turtle forward 10 spaces, 4 times.  In all, the turtle will move forward 40 spaces.
REPEAT 4 [FORWARD 10]
