LOGIN

On a Mac...

  1. Click on the Login Box in the lower right-hand corner of the screen.
  2. Click on the Login button.
  3. Type in your NetID (i.e. Firstname_Lastname) in the space provided.
  4. Type in your password in the space provided (capital and lowercase letters DO matter here).
  5. Click on the OK button and wait.

On an IBM...

  1. At the C:> prompt, type the word win and press Enter/Return. Wait for the teal screen and the login box.
  2. Click on the Login box.
  3. Type in your NetID in the space provided.
  4. Type in your password in the space provided.
  5. Press Enter/Return and wait.
