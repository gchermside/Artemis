Lessons

We spend part of each day in a computer lab, learning computer literacy through the use of programs such as Microsoft Powerpoint and Adobe Photoshop, or learning the basics of computer programming through web design in HTML and CSS, programs like AppInventor, and coding languages Python. We also spend part of each day in a classroom, where students will be introduced to the more theoretical aspects of computer science. During this portion of the day, we cover topics such as Cryptography and Artificial Intelligence through games, guest lectures, and other hands-on activities.
