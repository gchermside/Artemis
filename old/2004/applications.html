Applications for Artemis 2005 will be announced Spring 2005. Watch for upcoming changes in due dates. Below are the instructions for Artemis 2004 in case you would like to see a brief timeline.

How to apply:

To apply for Artemis, you must complete the Application. We also require one Teacher Recommendation as well. Both of these need to be postmarked by: April 21.

You will be notified regarding your acceptance by May 5.

How to contact us:

You can send us your application by post, fax, or e-mail.
To reach us by post, please mail to:

The Artemis Project 2004
Computer Science Department
Brown University
Box 1910
Providence, RI 02912-1910

To e-mail us, please click on the white envelope (labeled Artemis) which is located on each page of this website: artemis@cs.brown.edu.

Additionally, our contact information is on the application and in the brochure.
