How You Can Help!

The Planet needs lots of help cleaning up. Here are some ways you can help around your community!

  1. Don't litter! Throw your trash away in the garbage.
  2. Don't waste water! This helps save, and conserve water.
  3. Bring a trash bag to the Beach with you! This will prevent trash from getting into the water and becoming harmful to our environment.
  4. Join a beach cleanup to help a local beach in your area. This will make the beaches safer and more fun for everyone.
  5. Spread the word! Tell your friends about Water Pollution and how they can help. The more people that know about it, the more people help out!
