What is Water Pollution?

Water Pollution is an important issue that is so common, but is and affecting people all over the world. Roughly 700 million people in the world consume contaminated water. Every piece of litter or toxin will eventually get into water and can harm water sources and living things. Think about the people you see throwing trash out their car window while driving, or at the beach when you empty plastic bottle blew away. It is our job to spread the word about the harm of water pollution.
