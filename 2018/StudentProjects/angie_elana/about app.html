About the App

The Anti-Stereotyper is a stereotype based Jeopardy game. There are 4 categories to choose from: gender, race, and culture, disabilities all of which are common areas of stereotypes and discrimination.

After choosing a category, users will be presented with a multiple choice question, to determine which is the stereotype. Upon choosing the correct answer, they will be presented with background information on this stereotype and how it affects society today. They will also find information on people who have worked against the said stereotype and the discrimination it has caused, and ways that they themselves could work against it.
