Our Goal

The Anti-Stereotyper was created with two main goals: to raise awareness of stereotypes and to influence people to break them. By discovering how certain stereotypes affect society, potential users will be more inclined to work against them.

However, most users will be doubtful of how much influence they can have, or simply not know where to begin. The Anti-Stereoyper will show them how they can make a difference and hopefully encourage them to take action.
