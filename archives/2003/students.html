Instructions on how to get to the CIT
  1. Take bus up the hill to Thayer Street
  2. When dropped off, walk right, towards the tallest building on the street (Brown's Science Library)
  3. To the right of the library (as you're facing its steps) is the Thomas Watson Center for Information Technology, also known as the CIT
  4. Artemis is on the first floor on the right. Room 167, the MS lab

Regarding the waiver forms...
When parents sign the release and waiver forms, a witness *must also* sign it. A witness can be a neighbor, the other parent, a relative -- ANYONE who is over 18 and sees that the form has been signed by a parent.
