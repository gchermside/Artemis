We created Share-ities , to help volunteers find a charity that they will love, and help them make a positive impact on lives, near and far.

The goals of our app is to find YOU a charity that you will love. By using our app you can take a survey that will match you with one of the best charities ever! In order for these charities to do the amazing work that they are doing, they need people like YOU! Help make an impact by getting our app today!

Charities are in need of volunteers to help them expand and continue to work to make a difference.So many of us forget that there are large amounts of people less fortunate than we are. It is so easy to get involved in helping the inpoverished and underpriveleged. The experience is very rewarding!
