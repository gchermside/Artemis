All About Us!

Hi! We are the creators of this website! Here is a little bit about us and why we created this website:

We are beginner computer scientists who created this website at a Computer Science camp. Through this camp, we are able to see that we can create many things with all different types of code. Computer Science has inspired us because many people have made a huge impact on the world. Think about iPhones, computers, and your favorite app. Even Instagram and Snapchat are made by coders. All of these examples have been coded by Computer Scientists around the world. The world of Computer Science is huge and has endless opportunities. In the future, we hope to be able to see how Computer Science advances and maybe even be part of that advancement.

We created this website to spread the word about Water Pollution. We feel that this is a big issue in our world, and there needs to be more change in order to fix it. We want to inspire people to make a difference and help in the fight for cleaner and safer water.
