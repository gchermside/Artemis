How Kittens are More Charming

Kittens like to tumble around and play. Kittens playing is cuter than puppies playing because of their wide-eyed excitement at everything that moves. After kittens play, their sleepyness is cuter than any puppy. They curl up in a little furry ball for a nap on a cold day, and stretch out their tiny paws on a hot one.

In addition, kittens meow like little squeaky toys and flop around on their tiny little legs. They purr when pet, and are like little hot water bottles that chew on your hair. They scratch and climb and explore more than puppies do.

A Recap...

  • Kittens are more playful.
  • Kittens make cuter noises.
  • Kittens are more expressive in their movements.

Why Kittens have better physical traits

Kittens are smaller and slimmer than puppies, and more flexible. Therefore, they are easier to cuddle. Kittens are more skittish than puppies, and their adorable fur fluffs up a lot. They have tiny baby paws with toes that look like beans. They have tiny needle-sharp claws to grip things with. Kittens are generally more fluffy than puppies, and therefore cuter. Most kittens have little pointy ears, which many puppies lack. Therefore all physical traits point to kittens being smaller and more adorable.
