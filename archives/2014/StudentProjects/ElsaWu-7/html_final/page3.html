Personalities

Personalities differ from nation to nation, just like their stereotypes. Some are much older, earning them this slight babysitter outtake on the other, younger, nations(Ex: China). While some of the happier, imature nations(Ex; Italy and America) annoy the older more stodic ones(Ex: Germany and England).

Friendships(or in this case, alliances) between counties are either from the show, actual history, or fan based. Scored by how good the actual countries of the world get along with each other in the past or present. This information is also useful for shipping purposes as well.

Remember this:

  • Each nation has their own intrest as well. For example, America has his video games and Canada has his maple syrup.
  • Some counties might have the same traits and/or intrests. Like futbol(or soccer), or hockey.
  • All of them have a companion of sorts to either hang out with or make fun of. Like Italy and Germany or Sealand and Latvia. The relationship can be good, bad, or odd depanding on how their personalities clash.
