The Artemis Project 2002 First Day FAQ


Question Answer
What time should I get to camp? For the first day, you need to be at Kennedy Plaza between 8:30 - 9:00 am. Remember, most days you will need to be on campus by 9:00 am, and you should probrably leave yourself 15 minutes to walk up the hill from the bus stop. So the bus you take the first day may not be the bus you want to take the rest of the summer. Look into the schedules at RIPTA's website.
What if I miss the bus or something and lose the group? You can meet up with us at The Thomas J. Watson Center for Information Technology (commonly called the "C.I.T."), which is located at 115 Waterman Street. To get there from Kennedy Plaza, take the next bus up the hill to Thayer Street (if you have any problems finding which bus to take, ask any driver). After the bus drops you off on Thayer Street, walk right towards the tallest building on the street (the Sciences Library). Enter into the area where the library is located, and you should see on your right The Thomas J. Watson Center for Information Technology. Artemis is located on the fourth floor. If you get lost or have any trouble, find a phone and call 439-0926. If you reach the Brown campus, you can use any of the yellow phones on campus and call 3-7720.
Do I need a lunch? Nope. Remember, we'll provide you with lunch everday.
What do I need to bring? Don't forget the bus token we sent you for the bus ride here. Other than that, you should bring your folder that you either received at the dessert or in the mail and a pen or pencil.
What can't I bring?
  • Electronic communication devices such as beepers, pagers and cell phones
  • Electronic games
  • Walkmen, Discmen, MP3 players, or any other portable music device
  • Valuables such as large amounts of money or jewlery
  • Weapons of any kind including pocket knives.
How do I get home? When you get here we'll give you your summer bus pass. You'll probably take the same bus home that you took here. Look into the schedule to find the one that best fits with our camp schedule. Remember, our day ends at 3:30.
What are the camp hours? Monday - Friday, 9:00 am - 3:30 pm.
Do we have camp on the 4th of July? Nope. Nor do we have camp on the 5th of July. Enjoy the long weekend!
Is there an emergency number I should know? 401-863-7600. You don't really need to know it, but your parents might want it.
