Programs in LOGO
You can teach Logo how to draw a square or a circle by making it a PROGRAM.
TO <name of your program>
<command 1>
<command 2>
…
END
Every time you want to write a program it must be in this form:
For example:                                       name of the program
TO square
REPEAT 4 [FORWARD 100 RIGHT 90]
END
the commands
