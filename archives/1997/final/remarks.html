Artemis Remarks

Artemis is a summer Program for young girls going to high school. It takes place for a month in Brown University. The meaning for this program is so that us young girls will get the opportunity to make computers useful to us in life, and while we're at that, we also want to enjoy ourselves and make new friends. To tell you the truth, most people already knew each other already, but we all, those who didnt know each other, rather got acquintted quickly.
