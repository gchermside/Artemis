Water Pollution Game!

By seeing all of the Water Pollution around us, we wanted to make a difference. We created a game that raises awareness of Water Pollution. This game is called Can Collector. The game consists of a lake and trash like we ofter see floating in water. When the game starts, cans will begin to show up on the screen. It's the players job to collect as many pieces of trash as possible before 3 peices of trash touch the red line. This will result in the end of the game.
