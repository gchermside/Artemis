WordBlot was created as a final product for the Artemis Project, a five week program at Brown University that empowers young women to learn about computer science. Look below to learn more about Miracle and Emeline.

Miracle Cooper:

Miracle Cooper is one of the cofounders of WordBlot. She is from Brockton, Massachussets and lives with her two brothers and three sisters. Miracle's favorite subject in school is biology, and in her free time she likes to draw.


Emeline Ahn:

Emeline Ahn is the other cofounder of WordBlot. She is from Pawtucket, Rhode Island and lives with her older sister, Sophie. Emeline's favorite subject in school is math, and in her free time she likes to play the cello.
