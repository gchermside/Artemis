Nowadays, students are always on their smartphones or tablets, playing games, checking their social media, texting, playing games, or taking selfies. The list goes on and on. There are apps for everything. Do you want to learn how to cook? There is an app for that. Do you want to play a fake piano? There is an app for that. What about learning math using the classic word-guessing game Hangman? There is an app for that. We created this app. Introducing the all-new game Hang in There-Math!. Using a fun, easy-to-play game, this app will help students struggling with math and make it a fun and enjoyable experience.
